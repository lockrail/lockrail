# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Lockrail project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful and constructive
- accepting and giving technical feedback in good faith
- focusing on the security and reliability of the project
- showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or discriminatory language
- trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- publishing private information without permission
- conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, wiki edits,
and other contributions that do not align with these standards.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting

Report abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior to the project
maintainers through the security or maintainer contact published in
`SECURITY.md`.
